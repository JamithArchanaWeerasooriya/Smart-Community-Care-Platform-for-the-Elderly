Understanding the Domain

Setting reminders
Triggering emergency alerts
Finding helpers
Checking status
Cancelling actions
General greetings and confirmations

1. Intents

set_reminder - Add a new reminder for a specific task/time/date
update_reminder - Change or reschedule a reminder
delete_reminder - Delete a scheduled reminder
check_reminder - Check upcoming reminders
set_emergency_alert - Trigger emergency or ask for help
cancel_command - Cancel current process or action
find_helper - Find community helper or caregiver
check_status - Ask system about schedule, health, or day status
deny - Decline / disagree
thanks - Express gratitude

2. Entity

time - Time of event
date - Date/day
task - Task or activity
person - Helper or contact
location - Place if mentioned
context - Emergency or emotional state
